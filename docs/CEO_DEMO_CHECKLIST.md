# CEO Demo Checklist

## Demo goal

Show Call Master Enterprise IQ as an enterprise-ready command center for call quality, sales conversion, rejection recovery, live assist, coaching and governance.

## Demo sequence

1. Executive IQ
2. Sales Funnel
3. Rejection Funnel
4. Live Assist
5. AI Studio
6. Best Call Library
7. Critical Insights
8. Email Template Center
9. Coaching Calendar
10. SaaS Control
11. Enterprise Readiness
12. Client Portal

## Talking points

### Executive IQ

- Single leadership view.
- Revenue, quality, leakage and risk in one place.
- Process comparison and critical insight cards.

### Sales Funnel

- Customer sales transition visibility.
- Stage-wise leakage.
- Agent-level actioning.
- Live database/fallback banner.

### Rejection Funnel

- Customer rejection transition visibility.
- Reason intelligence.
- Recovery candidates.
- Coachability view.

### Live Assist

- Partial-production contract visible.
- Live score, event stream, checklist and next-best-action.
- Clear production gaps for streaming and supervisor whisper.

### AI Studio

- Prompt governance.
- Audit framework view.
- Model and confidence governance.
- Prompt endpoint partially database-backed.

### Best Call Library

- Curated winning calls.
- Coaching playlists.
- Best calls and playlists partially database-backed.

### Critical Insights

- Critical business alerts.
- Evidence, recommendation, owner and due date.

### Email Template Center

- Communication workflow concept.
- App-owned persistence required before production completion.

### Coaching Calendar

- Converts insights into coaching actions.
- App-owned calendar persistence required before production completion.

### SaaS Control

- Tenant and feature control concept.
- Tenant persistence still required.

### Enterprise Readiness

- Build, data and release readiness view.

### Client Portal

- External sharing concept.
- Permission model still required.

## Do not overclaim

- Do not say Live Assist is fully real-time until streaming is implemented.
- Do not say Email Template Center is production-complete until app-owned tables are created.
- Do not say Client Portal is production-ready until permissions are implemented.
- Do not hide fallback states.

## CEO close

Position the product as demo-ready and structurally enterprise-ready, with a clear roadmap for final production hardening.
